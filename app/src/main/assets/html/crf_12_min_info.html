About Cardvascular Stresstest

Why a stress test

It is used to determine how well your heart responds during times when it is working the hardest. Completing the test will provide data that scientists can use to assess the accuracy of digital versions of the tests.

What to do

  1. You’ll be hooked up to the EKG machine.
  2. Nurse will check your heart rate and breathing before you begin exercising.
  3. You’ll start off slowly on a stationary bicycle or treadmill. The speed and grade will be increased through the test duration.
  4. You will be asked to continue until you feel exhausted.
  5. Your heart rate and breathing will continue to be monitored for a short while after the test.
